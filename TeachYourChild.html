Heavy Rain

When it rains heavily, big drops fall fast, and the streets might fill with water. It can be fun to watch, but it’s important to stay safe and dry.

Tips for Rainy Weather:

  • Use an umbrella or raincoat if you go outside.
  • Wear waterproof shoes to keep your feet dry.
  • Don’t play in puddles or deep water — it might be dirty or slippery.
  • Stay inside if there’s thunder, lightning, or heavy rain.
  • Watch the rain from the window — sometimes you might see a rainbow after! 🌈

Very Cold Weather

When it’s very cold, the air feels freezing, and you might see your breath in the air! Sometimes it even snows in some places. Cold weather can make you sick if you don’t stay warm.

Tips for Cold Weather:

  • Wear warm clothes, like a jacket, scarf, gloves, and boots.
  • Drink warm drinks, like milk or cocoa, to stay cozy.
  • Stay inside when it’s too cold or windy.
  • Keep moving a little to stay warm, but don’t stay outside too long.
  • Cover your head and ears because they get cold fast.

Chapter 3: Windy Weather

Now let's know more about winds and how should we avoid it by following the episode.

Strong Wind

When the wind blows strongly, it can push things around — trees move, doors close suddenly, and light things like papers can fly away! Strong wind can also make it harder to walk or play outside.

Tips for Windy Weather:

  • Stay away from trees or signs that could fall.
  • Stay indoors if the wind is very strong.
  • Hold onto your hat or umbrella, or they might fly away!
  • Don’t fly kites or play with light toys when it’s too windy — it’s not safe.
  • Walk carefully, especially if things are blowing around.

Very Hot Weather

When the weather is very hot, the sun shines strongly, and the air feels warm everywhere. You might start to sweat or feel tired quickly. It’s important to protect your body from the heat.

Tips for Hot Weather:

  • Drink lots of water so you don’t get dehydrated.
  • Avoid playing outside in the middle of the day when the sun is the strongest.
  • Wear light clothes, a hat, and sunglasses if you go out.
  • Stay in the shade or inside a cool room whenever you can.
  • Eat light food like fruits to help your body stay cool.
